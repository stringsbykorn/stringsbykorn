Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know before booking.

When you book, let me know: the head brand/model, the service you want, your preferred pocket depth (low, mid, high), channel type (whippy vs. clean), any color preferences for mesh/string, and whether you need rush turnaround. The more detail you give, the better I can build exactly what you want.
I can string any standard men's lacrosse head — STX, StringKing, ECD, Maverik, Brine, Warrior, and more. If you have a less common head or a women's head, just reach out and we can discuss compatibility before booking.
Payment is collected at pickup (or before shipment for mail-in). I accept Venmo (@stringsbykorn) and Square (credit/debit card). No cash required, though it's accepted locally. Payment is due when you pick up your finished stick.
Yes — if a sidewall string breaks or a shooting string comes undone within 2 weeks of pickup due to a stringing defect (not misuse), I'll re-string that section for free. I stand behind my work.
During the off-season, turnaround is fast — usually 2–4 days. In the spring pre-season (February–March), slots fill up quickly. Book early, especially if you want standard turnaround. Rush add-on is available for urgent requests.
I keep an inventory of the most common mesh and string colors. I'll do my best to match your team colors — if I don't have a specific color in stock, I can order it (adds a few days to turnaround). DM me on Instagram if you want to check availability before booking.
